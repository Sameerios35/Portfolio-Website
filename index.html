About Me

Hello! I am a recent graduate from the University of Houston, having pursued my BS degree in Computer Science. At my time at UH, I completed a Dual Minor in Economics and Mathematics, broadening my knowledge and skills across disciplines. Throughout my journey, I engaged in various student organizations that played a crucial role in my personal and professional development. As the President of IEEE-NSM, I organized workshops that benefited students from diverse majors, emphasizing the importance of interdisciplinary collaboration. I achieved the distinction of graduating Magna cum laude, a testament to my dedication and academic excellence. My journey began in high school, where I earned an Associate of Science Degree while simultaneously managing high school courses, extracurricular activities, and sports. This honed my time management skills, allowing me to navigate a demanding schedule effectively. I am passionate about making a positive impact and guiding others. As I explore new opportunities, I remain curious and eager to learn, constantly seeking to expand my skill set. Feel free to message me!
